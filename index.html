An Angular question typically refers to an interview or technical question related to Angular, a popular TypeScript-based front-end web application framework developed by Google.

Examples of Angular Questions:

  • What is Angular, and how is it different from AngularJS?

  • What are components in Angular?

  • What is data binding in Angular?

  • How does Angular's dependency injection work?

  • What are directives, and how are they used?
